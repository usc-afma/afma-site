About

US

Bridge US-China Filmmakers

AFMA is the first and only USC-recognized student organization dedicating to film marketing and distribution practices. AFMA was founded in 2013 by USC students who share the mutual passion for film. We link filmmakers with film lovers, and the industry, aiming to create a platform where filmmakers can connect with each other, and communicate with professionals and insiders from other parts of the industry.

Our services include but are not limited to: film festivals, industry panels, networking mixers, film marketing, and etc. Since 2013, AFMA have organized four grand film festivals, notorious panels every month of the year, and several seasonal networking mixers, building a bridge between young filmmakers, film lovers, and industry professionals.
